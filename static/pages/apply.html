Co-operative Education Program - Application Agreement

By submitting this application, I, the applicant, acknowledge and agree to the following terms and conditions governing participation in the TMU Co-operative Education Program:


1. Accuracy of Information. I certify that all information provided in this application is accurate, complete, and true to the best of my knowledge. I understand that any misrepresentation may result in immediate rejection of my application or termination from the co-op program.


2. Academic Standing. I understand that acceptance into the co-op program is contingent on maintaining the required academic standing as set by the Co-op Office. Failure to maintain this standing may result in removal from the program.


3. Work Term Obligations. If accepted, I agree to fulfill all work term obligations, including submitting required reports by the specified deadlines (within 30 days of the end of each work term), participating in employer evaluations, and adhering to workplace policies.


4. Privacy and Data Use. I consent to the TMU Co-op Office collecting, storing, and processing my personal information for the purposes of administering the co-op program, including sharing relevant information with prospective employers.


5. Provisional Acceptance. I understand that this application results in provisional acceptance only. Final acceptance is subject to review of academic requirements and other criteria as determined by the Co-op Coordinator.


6. Code of Conduct. I agree to uphold the TMU's Code of Student Conduct during all co-op activities, including workplace placements. Violations may result in disciplinary action and removal from the program.


7. Amendment. The Co-op Office reserves the right to amend these terms and conditions at any time. Continued participation constitutes acceptance of any changes.
